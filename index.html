About Vertigo

Vertigo was formed in early 2006 by Nik Rincon with other members of his previous bands. The motto behind the band was to rock out the audiences of Texas to tunes that have caused major radio impact and live in the memories of many of us and are signature songs of the artist Vertigo covers. Vertigo's repertoire focuses on 80's power dance rock with a twist of modern pop, alternative rock and current rock hits delivered with energy and captivating emotion.

We hope to see you at one of our shows in the near future!
